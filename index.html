What is AFIRE ?

We are a group of Industry Professionals & Academic experts trying to bridge the gap between Industry employment needs and Engineering student’s Academic exposure. The Vision of the Organisation is to make engineering students industry ready thus enabling skill based employment opportunities.

Why AFIRE ?

• Only 30% of the academic learnings are being used by industry professionals
• 70% of the skills are being learnt during employment or employee ends up quitting the Job
• 45% of the Engineering graduates are working in non core domain
• 60% of the Engineering students spends around 8 months of time in domain based training after completing Engineering studies
• Only 20-25% of engineering students involve themselves in skill development related activities.

What do we do ?

Industry ready Training curriculum
Introduction to the course
● Need and Scope
● Design Thinking
● Case Studies
● Basics of Arduino
● I/O components
● Programming types
● Sample Projects
Conferences & Meets
• On demand Conferences can be conducted
• Tech meetups
• Bootcamps with entrepreneurs
• Tech Talks
Cross domain Projects and Hackathons
• Semester wise Hackathons will be conducted
• Cross domain projects will be made to empower students for real life project management
Mentors Network
• Gamified Network for Students to interact with mentors
• Credit based answering for mentors for engaging
• Platform for senior students/Experts to share and earn
SSP- Students Skill Profile
• Students skill profile Helps students to apply for the right job with right skills.
• First of its kind for college to map and analyse students realtime skillset
• Can be shared with Hr’s for inviting them for campus placements
• Helps students to showcase their expertise in interviews rather than a generic profile
